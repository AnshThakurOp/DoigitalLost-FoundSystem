Lost Prevention Tips

General Tips

  • Always keep your belongings secure and within sight.
  • Use a bag with zippers and keep it closed at all times.
  • Be mindful of your surroundings, especially in crowded places.
  • Don't leave your belongings unattended, even for a short period.
  • Use locks on your luggage when traveling.
  • Avoid carrying large sums of cash; use cards or electronic payment methods instead.

At Home

  • Install security cameras at entry points.
  • Keep doors and windows locked when not at home.
  • Store valuables in a safe or hidden location.
  • Be cautious when letting strangers into your home.
  • Consider using a home security system.
  • Keep track of spare keys and avoid hiding them in obvious places.

While Traveling

  • Keep important documents and valuables in a secure pouch or money belt.
  • Avoid displaying expensive items in public.
  • Be cautious of pickpockets in crowded tourist areas.
  • Use hotel safes to store valuable items.
  • Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi networks; avoid accessing sensitive information.
  • Keep a photocopy of important documents in a separate location.

Additional Tips

  • Be aware of scams and distractions used by thieves.
  • Report any suspicious activity to authorities.
  • Consider using tracking devices for valuable items.
  • Regularly review your financial statements for unauthorized transactions.
  • Be cautious when sharing personal information online.
  • Keep emergency contact numbers handy in case of theft or loss.
